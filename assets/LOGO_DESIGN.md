# Wing Browser Logo Design

## Design Concept

The Wing Browser logo embodies the spirit of digital freedom, transformation, and Rwandan identity through a stylized butterfly motif that forms a "W" shape.

## Design Principles

### Modern & Clean
- Smooth curves and gradients create a contemporary feel
- Minimalist approach ensures clarity at all sizes
- Professional appearance suitable for a serious software product

### Culturally Relevant
- Colors reflect Rwandan national identity
- Butterfly is a universal symbol of transformation, resonating with Rwanda's development story
- Design is modern while respecting cultural values

### Scalable
- Vector format (SVG) ensures perfect quality at any size
- Simplified versions maintain recognizability when small
- Works well in both light and dark contexts
